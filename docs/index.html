Chariot.js

An extremely lightweight, unopinionated and extensible Eris client framework

Get Started →

Unopinionated

Chariot.js doesn't force a certain style on you. There really is nothing you have to use out of the box. You can organize, structure and build your commands and modules exactly as you want.

Robust

Chariot.js completely takes care of command and event module handling with absurd amounts of detail and provides assets for even the most tricky of situations you may face developing.

Performant & Secure

Tested, used and loved by many large bots in every day situations, Chariot.js provides an extremely performant and secure entry into the exciting world of developing Discord bots with Eris.
